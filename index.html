What is TAS?

Technology and Applied Studies (TAS) is a key learning area (KLA) that helps students to understand design principles and processes, problem-solving and the development of practical skills.

Stage 4

The technology subject is mandatory for all students which allows learners to experience a range of topic areas, thus assisting them when choosing between subjects in the senior years of high school.

Subjects

There are many technology and design-related topics which can be studied through a variety of courses. Some examples include Food Technology, Design and Technology and Textiles and Design.
