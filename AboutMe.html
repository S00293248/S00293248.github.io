About Me

My name is Victoria Zambetti and I am a second year student studying a Bachelor of Education (Secondary)/Bachelor of Arts (Technology)

My Goals and Experiences

My goal is to teach Food Technology amongst other Technology/TAS subjects as I have an eagerness to educate and help those around me.

Through my experiences as a tutor and cadet leader I have developed an understanding of what it means to motivate and encourage students to achieve their full potential. Working as a School Learning Support Officer in a high school further encouraged me to pursue a career in education and ignited a passion for teaching.

I studied food technology throughout high school and gained a great level of understanding in cooking techniques, food development, nutrition and the role of food in cultural celebrations. I developed a deep understanding of the importance of food knowledge. My fondest childhood memories involve learning to cook from my family members and celebrations with a great emphasis on food.

My future goals as a TAS teacher are to prepare young people to find independence by educating them in the skills and knowledge to lead a healthy lifestyle and pass on these capabilities to their families or apply them to the workforce.
